## Key Year 4 Milestones

### English (Lower KS2)
- **Reading:** Justifying inferences with evidence, summarising main ideas
- **Writing:** Fronted adverbials, improved cohesion, proof-reading
- **Grammar:** Apostrophes for plural possession, varied sentence structures
- **Spoken Language:** Presentations, performances, debates

### Mathematics (Year 4)
- **Times Tables:** ALL tables to 12×12 (Multiplication Tables Check in June)
- **Place Value:** Four-digit numbers (thousands)
- **Decimals:** Introduction to tenths and hundredths
- **Fractions:** Equivalent fractions
- **Written Methods:** Formal methods for multiplication
- **New Concepts:** Area, perimeter, coordinates

### Science (Lower KS2)
- **New Topics:** States of Matter, Sound, Electricity
- **Living Things:** Classification keys
- **Human Body:** Digestive system, teeth
- **Physics:** Solids/liquids/gases, water cycle, circuits
