## Key Year 5 Milestones

### English (Upper KS2)
- **Reading:** Evaluating authors' language use, distinguishing fact from opinion
- **Writing:** Identifying audience and purpose, using cohesive devices
- **Grammar:** Relative clauses, modal verbs, parenthesis with brackets/dashes
- **Spoken Language:** Articulating complex arguments and opinions

### Mathematics (Year 5)
- **Place Value:** Numbers to at least 1,000,000
- **Operations:** Formal written methods for larger numbers
- **Fractions & Decimals:** Mixed/improper fractions, decimals to thousandths
- **Percentages:** Introduction to % as parts per hundred
- **New Concepts:** Negative numbers through zero, prime numbers, volume

### Science (Upper KS2)
- **New Topics:** Earth and Space, Forces, Properties and Changes of Materials
- **Living Things:** Life cycles (mammals, amphibians, insects, birds), reproduction
- **Human Development:** Changes from birth to old age
- **Physics:** Gravity, air/water resistance, friction, planetary motion
- **Chemistry:** Solubility, reversible vs irreversible changes

## Start of Upper KS2

Year 5 begins Upper Key Stage 2:
- More abstract scientific concepts
- Larger numbers and more complex calculations
- Greater emphasis on inference and analysis in reading
- Increasing independence in learning
- Preparation for end of KS2 SATs in Year 6
