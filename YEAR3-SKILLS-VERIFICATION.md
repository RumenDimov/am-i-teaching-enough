## Key Year 3 Milestones

### English (Lower KS2)
- **Reading:** Inference, prediction, main ideas from multiple paragraphs
- **Writing:** Paragraphs, varied sentence structures, direct speech
- **Spelling:** Prefixes/suffixes (un-, dis-, mis-, re-)
- **Grammar:** Conjunctions (when, if, because, although), present perfect tense

### Mathematics (Year 3)
- **Place Value:** Numbers to 1000
- **Times Tables:** 3, 4, 8 (in addition to 2, 5, 10)
- **Written Methods:** Column addition/subtraction
- **Fractions:** Tenths, unit and non-unit fractions
- **Measurement:** Standard units (cm, m, kg, g, l, ml)
- **Time:** 12-hour and 24-hour clock

### Science (Lower KS2)
- **New Topics:** Rocks, Light, Forces & Magnets
- **Plants:** Functions of parts, water transport
- **Animals:** Nutrition, skeletons, muscles
- **Working Scientifically:** Systematic observations, measurements
