## Key Year 2 Milestones

### English
- **Phonics:** Secure decoding skills, passing Year 1 Phonics Check standard
- **Reading:** Fluent reading of age-appropriate books
- **Writing:** Narratives, information texts, using conjunctions
- **Grammar:** Sentence types, commas, apostrophes

### Mathematics
- **Number:** Secure understanding to 100
- **Times Tables:** 2, 5, 10 times tables known
- **Operations:** Add/subtract to 100, multiply/divide
- **Fractions:** 1/2, 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 3/4
- **New:** Statistics (charts, graphs)

### Science
- **Living Things:** Habitats, food chains
- **Plants:** Growth from seeds/bulbs
- **Animals:** Life cycles, nutrition, exercise
- **Materials:** Uses and properties

---

## End of KS1 Assessment

Year 2 is the statutory assessment year for KS1:
- **Phonics Screening Check** (if not passed in Year 1)
- **Teacher Assessment** in Reading, Writing, Maths, Science
- **Optional SATs** (since 2023, no longer statutory)
