## Key Year 6 Milestones

### English (Upper KS2)
- **Reading:** Advanced inference, evaluating language and structure, literary analysis
- **Writing:** Formal writing for varied audiences, advanced cohesion, revision skills
- **Grammar:** Passive voice, semicolons, colons, expanded noun phrases
- **Spoken Language:** Formal debates and presentations, Standard English

### Mathematics (Year 6)
- **Place Value:** Numbers to 10,000,000, negative numbers
- **Operations:** Long multiplication and division, BIDMAS/BODMAS
- **Fractions:** Different denominators, multiplying and dividing fractions
- **Ratio & Algebra:** Introduction to ratio, proportion, simple formulae
- **New Concepts:** Four quadrant coordinates, pie charts, volume of cuboids

### Science (Upper KS2)
- **New Topics:** Evolution & Inheritance, Light, Circulatory System
- **Living Things:** Classification systems, vertebrates/invertebrates, microorganisms
- **Human Body:** Circulatory system, heart, blood vessels, lifestyle impacts
- **Evolution:** Fossils, adaptation, natural selection
- **Physics:** Light traveling in straight lines, circuit symbols

## End of Key Stage 2

Year 6 completes primary education:
- End of KS2 SATs assessments (May)
- Transition to secondary school
- Expected to read and write with secondary-level fluency
- Foundation for KS3 curriculum
- Emphasis on independence and resilience
