## Key Stage 1 Assessment

At the end of Year 2 (not Year 1), pupils are assessed against National Curriculum standards in:
- **English Reading** - Phonics Screening Check in Year 1, formal assessment in Year 2
- **English Writing** - Teacher assessment
- **Mathematics** - Teacher assessment and optional tests
- **Science** - Teacher assessment (sampling, not all pupils)

Year 1 focuses on building foundations, with the main formal assessment being the **Phonics Screening Check** in June.

- KS1 SATs became **optional** from **2023** (previously statutory)
